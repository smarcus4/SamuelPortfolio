Hobbies

I am passionate about aviation and aeronautics. I am passionate learning more about commercial, military, and general aviation planes. I am in pursuit of my pilots license and currently am comfortable with the Cessna 172 and Archer Piper.

I own a 10'' Dobsonian telescope that I use for some deep space and solar system viewing. I was a TA for Astronomy 113-114 (Stars, Galaxies, and the Universe) at George Mason University. I am also the Social Media Coordinator for the Northern Virginia Astornomy Club (NOVAC)

I enjoy riding my road bike along the W&OD trail, usually travelling out east toward Washington, DC. The furthest I have biked was 50 miles to Washington DC and back home. I also enjoy playing softball recreationally, tennis, basketball, and golf.

I started playing piano at the age of eight on my first electric keyboard and I eventually went on to explore percussion in middle school and guitar for four years in high school. I am currently using software to produce music using my Nektar LX 49+ and Bitwig DAW. Over the course of my 9 year instruction in piano, I earned 3 consecutive Superior trophies at competition.

I enjoy competitive video games because of the strategy, fun, and teamwork needed to beat the other team. I mostly play on my PC, but I also still play games on my xbox 360. I also enjoy board games including Catan, Word on the Street, Trivial Pursuit, and many more!
